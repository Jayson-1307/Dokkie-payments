Welcome to Dokkie

Dokkie is a platform that helps you to organize your outings with friends, family, or colleagues.

You can create a new outing, invite people, and keep track of who is coming, and who spents what.

You can also see an overview of all your outings and who is coming to each outing, with the amount of money that everyone paid.
